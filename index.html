About Me

Blockchain developer, security auditor, and builder of things.

I'm a Full-Stack Blockchain Developer and Smart Contract Security Auditor based in New York City. My journey started at the Columbia FinTech Bootcamp, where I built a foundation in Python, forecasting models, algorithmic trading, and smart contracts. Since then, I've built organizations, shipped products, and gone deep into DeFi, AI, and security.

I founded GitHat — an auth and hosting platform replacing Clerk + Vercel with micro front-ends and LLM-powered agents. I built NFTeria, a full DeFi + AI ecosystem with smart contract factories, Vyper contracts, ElizaOS agents, and ML-powered forecasting bots. I created ClickReserve for service-industry scheduling and SebasTN for AI applications.

I write smart contracts in Solidity, Vyper, and Clarity (Stacks), audit them with Foundry fuzzing and manual review, and build autonomous ML trading systems for GMX and ALPACA. I work with LangChain, ElizaOS, and RAG models to create AI agents that are both secure and useful. Looking ahead, I'm building toward Layer 2 solutions in Rust.
